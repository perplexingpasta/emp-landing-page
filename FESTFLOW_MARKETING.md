## CONTEXT: THE PRODUCT

**FestFlow** is a done-for-you fest website platform built specifically for Indian medical college intercollegiate fests. The developer builds the entire website for the college — the organiser touches zero code.

**Live production deployment:** https://www.tatvam2026.in/ (Tatvam 2026, JSS Medical College, Mysuru)

**Proven metrics from Tatvam 2026 (use these in copy):**
- 1,768 delegates registered
- ₹7.97 lakhs in transactions handled
- 99.9% uptime during fest
- 8,846 website visitors
- 26,036 page views

**What FestFlow includes:**
For delegates: Unique Delegate Registration IDs, Registration Tiers/Kits, Event Registration Cart Checkout, WhatsApp Groups & Community, Fast & Responsive website, Confirmation Emails, Live Fest Schedule, Merch Store

For organisers: Data Export (Cultural, Sports, Merch), Google Sheets Auto-Sync, Payment Verification, Marketing Emails, Data Analytics, Zero Data Loss, Custom Domain, Live Admin Dashboard, No Duplicate Registrations

**Target buyer:**
- Indian MBBS student (2nd–4th year)
- Role: Cultural Secretary, Fest Coordinator, Technical Head, or Fest Organisation Committee member
- Organising an intercollegiate medical college fest (50–2000+ delegates, 5–30 colleges)
- Has limited time (MBBS postings, exams, viva)
- Has limited technical knowledge
- Status-conscious — the fest reflects on their college's reputation
- Primary communication: WhatsApp
- Has experienced the pain of Google Forms / Unstop / manual registration

**The 3 core emotional desires (priority order):**
1. **Prestige** — "Our fest website looks incredible. Other colleges respect this."
2. **Peace of mind** — "I don't have to babysit the technical side. It just works."
3. **Control & clarity** — "I know exactly what's happening with registrations at all times."

**The 3 core fears:**
1. Registration chaos — wrong events, lost entries, messy delegate data
2. A cheap-looking website that embarrasses the college in front of peer institutions
3. Being bombarded with "how do I register?" queries on WhatsApp at midnight
